About our School

PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya Tinsukia, located in Assam, is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education to students. Our school is part of the Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan, which is known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development. We offer a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our experienced faculty, modern facilities, and diverse extracurricular activities ensure that every student receives a well-rounded education. We strive to instill values of integrity, respect, and lifelong learning in our students, preparing them to be responsible global citizens.
